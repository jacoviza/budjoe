# What are the fields that need to be retrieved from the receipts and invoices?
1. Date and time
2. Name of the merchant
3. Sub-total of the bill (before taxes)
4. Total of the bill
5. Taxes (ITBIS, IVA, etc)

# How each field should look like
This section will briefly explain the content of each field

## Date and time
Could be in many different formats. Sometimes the time portion will not be present. 

## Name of the merchant
It is normally the first line of the receipt or invoice and is written in a slightly different font size or build.

## Sub-total of the bill (before taxes)
Self-explanatory

## Total of the bill
This should be the largest amount printed in the receipt or invoice.

## Taxes (ITBIS, IVA, etc)
Self-explanatory
